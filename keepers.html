Keeper Costs

Cost: the first time you keep a player, it costs the round he was drafted (or round 10 if that was round 11+ or a waiver pickup). Each year you keep him after that, the cost climbs one round (never better than round 1).
Limit: each team may keep up to 2 players.
Eligibility: a player kept two seasons in a row (by the same team) can't be kept again — he goes back in the draft.
kept ’25 = already a keeper last season, so keeping him again is a repeat (cost climbs).
Offensive players only (kickers/defenses/IDP not shown).

Built with the Yahoo Fantasy API · costs update every few hours
